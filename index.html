Our Activities

Welcome to our beautiful lakeside vacation! We are so excited to have you here and hope that you enjoy your stay with us. To start off your vacation, why not take a peaceful boat ride on the lake or go for a nature walk to explore the surrounding area? If you're feeling a bit more relaxed, you can sit back and enjoy the lake breeze from the comfort of your room, sipping on a cold drink from one of our glasses. Whatever you choose to do, we hope that you have a wonderful and memorable time at Lake Baringo.

  • Boat Riding

    There is nothing quite like a boat ride early in the morning to start the day off right. As the sun rises over the horizon, the water comes alive with the sound of birds chirping and the peaceful lapping of waves against the boat. While on the ride, you might have the chance to spot some hippos and crocodiles basking in the sun, or even catch a glimpse of a beautiful island in the lake. As the ride continues into the afternoon, the sun begins to dip lower in the sky, casting a warm glow over the water. In the evening, the cool breeze off the lake and the sound of the boat's engine create a serene atmosphere, making it the perfect time to sit back and relax.

  • Bird Watching

    Bird watching along the shore of Lake Baringo in the morning and evening can be a delightful experience. The lake is home to a variety of birds, including many species of waterfowl and waders. In the morning, you may be able to spot herons and egrets hunting for fish in the shallows, while in the evening you might see flocks of pelicans and cormorants roosting on the shoreline. As the sun sets, you may also have the opportunity to view nocturnal birds such as owls and nightjars. In addition to bird watching, you can also try feeding the fish eagles that can be found around the lake. These majestic birds are a sight to behold as they dive into the water to catch their prey. Whether you are a seasoned birder or just starting out, Lake Baringo is an excellent place to observe and appreciate the beauty of nature.

    Book Now

  • Room's Booking & Camping

    The Soi Safari Lodge is a beautiful retreat located along the shore of Lake Baringo. The rooms at the lodge offer breathtaking views of the lake and the surrounding wilderness. Each room is spacious and tastefully decorated, with comfortable beds, plush linens, and modern amenities. The lodge also has a fantastic swimming pool with sparkling water that is perfect for a refreshing dip or a pool party. For a more rustic experience, the lodge also has a camping area with tents that have bonfires at night on an island offshore. The bonfires provide a cozy and intimate setting for guests to relax and unwind after a day of exploring the area. In addition to the Soi Safari Lodge, there are several other hotels and accommodations along the shore of Lake Baringo. The Royal Dove Hotel has good cottages that face the lake and a swimming pool for guests to enjoy. The Tumbili Cliff Hotel also has a swimming pool and offers comfortable cottages with stunning views of the lake. The Sandai Resort is another option with good rooms and a swimming pool. For a unique and more personal experience, there is also an Airbnb called the Lake House that is located along the shore of the lake. No matter which accommodation you choose, you will be able to enjoy the beauty and tranquility of the lake while also having access to modern amenities and activities..

  • Po099kot & Njemp's Village Visit

    Touring the Pokot and Njemp communities along the shore of Lake Baringo offers a unique and enriching cultural experience. These communities are home to diverse and vibrant cultures, with a rich history and traditions that have been passed down through generations. The people of these communities are known for their warm and welcoming nature, and visitors are often greeted with smiles and traditional dances. The Pokot and Njemp people have a deep connection to the land and the lake, and they rely on its resources for their livelihoods. Many of the people in these communities have dark skin tones and often wear beads around their necks as a form of traditional dress. These beads are often brightly colored and intricately designed, and they are a symbol of the community's cultural identity. Visiting these communities and learning about their traditions and way of life is a truly rewarding and enriching experience.

    Book Now

  • Nature Walk

    A nature walk along Lake Baringo is a great way to experience the beauty and diversity of Kenya's wildlife and landscape. The lake is home to a wide variety of birds, including pelicans, flamingos, and many other species. You may also see monkeys swinging through the trees or playing along the shores of the lake. The Snake Park at Lake Baringo is a popular attraction, where you can learn about different species of snakes and see them up close. In addition to the wildlife, the Tugen Hills provide a beautiful backdrop for the nature walk, with their towering peaks and sweeping views. Whether you are an avid birdwatcher or just looking to enjoy some time in nature, a nature walk along Lake Baringo is a must-do activity.

    Book Now

  • School Trips

    School children and researchers visiting Lake Baringo can participate in educational talks about the formation of the lake and the fishing activities that take place there. The lake was formed millions of years ago as a result of tectonic activity, and it is now home to a diverse array of fish species, including tilapia and catfish. During the educational talks, students can learn about the different methods of fishing that are used on the lake, such as handlining and netting. In addition to the educational talks, visitors can also participate in a nature walk around the lake, where they can observe the local flora and fauna and learn about the ecosystem of the lake. After the nature walk, the school children can enjoy a boat ride on the lake, where they can take in the stunning views and possibly spot some of the local wildlife. In the evening, they can try some local cuisine, such as roasted goat meat, which is a popular dish in the region. Overall, the trip to Lake Baringo is a great opportunity for students to learn about the environment and culture of the area while having fun and making lasting memories.
